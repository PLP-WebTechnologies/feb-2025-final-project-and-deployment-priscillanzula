Discover Water Sources

Find water sources near you using our GPS-enabled map. Filter by type (e.g., well, tap, river) or safety level (safe, unsafe, untested).

Click on a water source marker to view details such as:

  • Water source type (e.g., river, borehole)
  • Safety status
  • Distance from your location
